Projects

DOTA 2 Drafter

2018 - 2018

  • Apache Spark + ML
  • Keras
  • Python

Developed a utility for assisting players with DOTA 2 drafting. Along with 2 other team members, I developed a neural network with Keras as well as a Spark Multilayer Perceptron Classifier to predict the winning team based on draft compositions in a DOTA 2 game. This neural network, coupled with a Upper Confidence Bounds Monte Carlo Tree Search algorithm, provided draft suggestions to users to maximize winrate. We trained our UCT MCTS and neural networks off of an OpenDota data dump.

M3R-CY

2016 - 2017

  • Discord.py
  • MongoDB
  • Python

M3R-CY was a moderation assistance program developed for the /r/Overwatch discord server. It provided functionalities such as message filtering, role mutes, information queries, logs, and other utilities. During its tenure as the main moderation utility, coupled with LogBot, it was used by the server's staff to manage approximately 156,000 total and 30,000 concurrent users. Using a MongoDB database, it tracked nickname and name changes, dates of joins and leaves, and other pieces of information not accessible with the vanilla client. Discbots
